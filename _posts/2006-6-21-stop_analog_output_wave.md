Method stops the analog output wave.

### Definition 

```python
stop_analog_output_wave(channel: int)
```

### Required Arguments

* `channel: int` DAQ pin number. For example, channel 'AO0' is Analog Output pin `0`. There are two channels: `0` and `1`.
